Why Board & Training school is the best solution for your dog or puppy?

Many people choose private lessons in their home. That's good, very good, but then they either expect a miracle or think that their dog will be trained after one or two lessons. No, that will never happen. It will take weeks or months to train a dog. Another common scenario is that the owner gets busy or the trainer gets busy so the schedule will not be maintainable. After this occurs, the owner will hire another trainer. But again, this will only be for one or two lessons so their dog will never get properly trained. This leads to behavior problems never becoming solved. Basic obedience is a must for any dog. Only after this initial step, can one start solving other problems.

Behavior Specialist (Behaviorist)

Hi I am Dante, in dogs world better known as “Dee” or “Dee Dee” I have 30 years of experience training dogs. 98% of dog trainers use an Electric Collar for training. By using e-collars extensively, it can lead to permanent brain damage in dogs. I do not use any abusive methods. I trained dogs before the e-collar was even invented.

I have over 30 years of experience training a range of dogs— from little spoiled house dogs to aggressive pitbulls to police dogs. I understand dog psychology.

I rehabilitate and re-train dogs, after a bad trainer didn’t train them correctly.

Any dog is trainable, if you know how. And I know how.

Off Leash Training

Many trainers offer “Off leash” training to impress their customers. What is off leash training actually? Electric Collars! Again, abusing your dog with an e-collar. How else will you maintain control if you don’t have your dog on a leash? This is total nonsense.

I prefer to have my dog on a leash to maintain the control naturally.

I am not a new YouTube dog trainer like the ones who put an e-collar on a dog, pressing a button and when a dog almost kills him, he returns the dog back to the owner with the words "this dog is not trainable." What a clown. As I mentioned before, I have over 30 years of experience training dogs. I can achieve anything I want with any dog.

I can definitely help you with your dog, no matter what the problem is.

What I don't do

  • I don't train circus dogs
  • I don't train with clickers—you forget your clicker at home, then what? Click with your mouth? *click, click* Like an Idiot?
  • I don't train with electric collars—you can permanently damage your dog's brain
  • No bull, just pure natural training the right way—your dog will understand
